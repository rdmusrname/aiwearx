##  Early Disease Detection with AI

One of the most groundbreaking applications of AI in smartwatches is early disease detection. The U.S. Department of Defense has recently developed a wearable device  that utilizes  AI algorithms to identify diseases up to six days before symptoms appear. This remarkable invention has the potential  to revolutionize the healthcare industry, allowing for timely intervention and treatment.  To learn more about this cutting-edge technology, read the article [here](https://studyfinds.org/wearable-device-ai-disease/).

Parkinson's disease a neurodegenerative disorder, has also been  a focus of  AI-powered smartwatch research. Researchers have  discovered that by analyzing smartwatch data using  AI  tools, it is possible to detect Parkinson's disease at an early stage, even before the  appearance of hallmark symptoms. This breakthrough  not  only  enables early intervention but also provides valuable insights into the  progression of the disease. To delve deeper into this topic, refer to the articles [here](https://mpost.io/ai-and-smartwatches-can-detect-parkinsons-disease-early/) and [here](https://interestingengineering.com/health/smart-watches-could-detect-parkinsons-seven-years-before-clinical-diagnosis).
